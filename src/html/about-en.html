How I came into development

In 2019, I learned that a new set of classes for children is being opened on the site of the old registry office, which it is called the children's technopark Inventarium.

It became very interesting for me to try myself in this business, because additional education for me I've always been interested.

After the first programming lessons with a teacher, I was offered to start my own project, so that I could understand programming better, as well as to present my project at competitions.

First project defenses

The first times I defended the project in front of deputies of the Moscow region, before members of the State Duma, in front of members of various juries.

The project I performed with was Babka On The Hunt. The first major project I took part in participation – The Baltic competition, as a result of which he took second place.

The following contests were held at various venues in the Moscow region and the city of Moscow. I continued take part in competitions and develop your project.
